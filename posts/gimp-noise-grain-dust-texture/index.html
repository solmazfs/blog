Gimp Create Noise/Grain/Dust Texture Aug 2, 2022 69 Words in progress

"How to make procedural noise/grain/dust texture with gimp."

Create new document: - 720x720 & 300dpi - Fill with: Transparency

Filters > Render > Noise > Solid Noise - X/Y Size: 16, Detail: 15, Tileable: x, Random seed: 8

Colors > Threshold - 43x255

Filters > Noise > CIE Ich Noise - Dulling: 1, Lightness: 100, Chroma: 0, Hue: 0, Random seed: 3

More noise > Pick -> play around

Tweaking these may help you get better results.
