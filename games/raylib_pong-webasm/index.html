Pong - Smash! _Ver.0.9

Press **[Enter / Space]** to start the game.

Move paddles using **[Up]** and **[Down]** keys.

Hold **[Left Shift]** to slow down paddle movement.

Press **[Space]** to activate smash ability.

In gameplay, press **[P]** to switch between player control and AI play.

*Score system is currently undergoing maintenance!
